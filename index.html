About Gridline Studio

Who we are and how we work

Gridline Studio is a modern development agency based in Lagos, Nigeria. We specialize in building scalable React applications and digital products that solve real business problems. Our team combines technical expertise with design thinking to deliver solutions that users love.

Founded by Michael Omale, a frontend developer and UI/UX designer with a background in Computer Science from Bells University of Technology, we've grown into a team of passionate developers and designers committed to excellence in every project.

We believe great software is built through collaboration, attention to detail, and a deep understanding of both technology and user needs. Every line of code we write and every interface we design reflects our commitment to quality and innovation.
